ABOUT US

Timeless Design

We design our furniture in-house taking inspiration from the enduring design movements of the past, particularly Shaker, Asian, Danish, Arts & Crafts and Modern. These eras share principles we consider fundamental to good design, like honoring natural materials, working with simple, organic forms and making the functional beautiful. Our designs are rooted in this heritage.
